Privacy Policy – AI Chart Intelligence

Effective Date: 23 November 2025

1. Data We Collect

Users can capture charts from webpages and generate insights.

  • Automatically collected: None. We do not track browsing history.
  • User-generated: Captured chart images and AI insights derived from them.

2. How We Use Data

Selected chart images are sent to our AI API to produce insights displayed in the extension. Data remains local unless explicitly exported.

3. Data Storage

Charts and summaries are stored in your browser using chrome.storage.local. They persist until reset or deletion.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension uses an AI service hosted at https://render.com/ for processing. Data sent is not used to train models.

5. User Control

You can reset all data anytime or uninstall the extension to delete stored content.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data locally. No personal info is collected.

7. GenAI Content

Insights are AI-generated and may not perfectly reflect the visual's intent. Verify before acting on them.

8. Changes

Privacy Policy updates will be reflected in the extension description.
